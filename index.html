VacMon

VacMon provides real-time monitoring and historical views of Vac and Vcycle resources. VM factories send updates to VacMon using the factory_status and machinetype_status messages in the VacQuery protocol. VacMon stores the contents of the messages in an ElasticSearch database. VacMon then presents graphical and tabular summaries of the messages on this website.

The All Sites view is the top level entry point to the system.
